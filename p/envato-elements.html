Questions about Envato Elements

What do I get?

Unlimited downloads: video templates, graphics, fonts, music, WordPress themes — everything a creator needs.

License on shared?

Downloads licensed to the account holder — for client work needing your name, choose Personal.

Can I use the images commercially? For Fiverr/client work?

Yes — Midjourney, Leonardo, Ideogram, Canva Pro and Adobe Firefly all permit commercial use on paid plans. Your Fiverr thumbnails, client logos, and social media posts are fully yours. The only caveat: AI-generated images cannot be copyrighted in most jurisdictions yet — but for freelance work, this has never been a practical issue.

Which image AI handles Bangla text best?

Ideogram is the current leader for text-in-image, including Bangla/English bilingual designs. Midjourney struggles with text; Leonardo is decent. For Bangla posters, use Ideogram for the text layer and Midjourney/Leonardo for the visuals — or use Canva Pro for templates.

Do I get to keep downloaded assets after my subscription ends?

Yes — assets downloaded during an active subscription carry a lifetime license. You can use them in projects indefinitely. You only need an active subscription to download new assets.
