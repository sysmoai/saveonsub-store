FAQ

Can I pay OpenAI directly with bKash?

No — OpenAI only accepts international cards. Bangladeshis use trusted resellers (bKash send-money), virtual dollar cards, or relatives abroad.

Is it safe to buy ChatGPT from a reseller with bKash?

It can be — if the reseller has a real website, order IDs, a written warranty and honest plan labels. Avoid anyone selling 'lifetime' plans or rushing you.

What is pay-after-testing?

A trust feature pioneered for first orders: the reseller sends access before you pay, you verify it works, then pay within an hour. SAVEONSUB offers it on most instant products.
