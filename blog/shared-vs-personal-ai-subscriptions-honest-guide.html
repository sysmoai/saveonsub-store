FAQ

What's the difference between shared and personal subscriptions?

Shared = one subscription split among users (70-85% cheaper, violates provider ToS, can reset — warranty covers). Personal = your own account (near-official price, zero risk).

Will other people see my data on a shared plan?

No — on ChatGPT, Midjourney and similar tools each user's content stays private. What's shared is the cost, not your data.

Which AI providers ban shared accounts most?

Anthropic (Claude) enforces most aggressively; OpenAI does periodic resets; Google and Canva are most tolerant. Honest resellers label this per product.
