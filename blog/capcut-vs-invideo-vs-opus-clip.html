FAQ

What is the best video editing app for Reels in Bangladesh?

CapCut Pro (৳399) for editing footage you already have — no watermark, 4K export, auto-captions. Its free tier is enough for beginners.

Invideo or Opus Clip — which is better?

Different jobs: Invideo generates video from a script (good for faceless channels); Opus Clip cuts long videos into short clips (good for podcasters).

Do these work with Bangla?

Invideo handles Bangla scripts; CapCut is a language-agnostic editor; Opus Clip works from captions.
