Coursera vs free YouTube — is paying worth it?

We sell Coursera. We will still tell you when NOT to buy it.

Buy Coursera when…

You need a verifiable certificate for your CV or a job/visa application, you want structured university-grade courses with graded assignments, or you are binge-learning across many courses (Plus = unlimited). For certificates in your own name, take a Personal plan — shared prints the account-holder's name.

Stick with free YouTube when…

You just want to learn a skill (no cert needed), you are exploring casually, or you are studying one specific topic. For 1–2 formal courses, apply for Coursera financial aid — it is generous and free. Honestly, most BD learners do not need Plus.

The BD-smart move

Free YouTube + free Coursera financial aid covers most people. Buy Coursera Plus only if certificates and volume genuinely matter to you.

FAQ

Is Coursera Plus worth it in Bangladesh?

Only if you need verifiable certificates or binge many courses. For casual learning, free YouTube plus Coursera's free financial aid (for 1-2 courses) is smarter.

Do Coursera certificates show my name?

On a personal plan, yes. On a shared plan they print the account-holder's name — useless for your CV. Take Personal if you need certificates.

Can I get Coursera courses free?

Yes — Coursera offers financial aid per course, which is generous. Apply free before paying for Plus.
