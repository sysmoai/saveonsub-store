# Review System — earning real reviews (replaces the "earning them" placeholder honestly)

> Brand law #1: no fake reviews, ever. This is how real ones get collected, verified, and published — and how the site upgrades when we have them.

## Collection (automatic via WHATSAPP-FLOWS §7)
T+7 after clean delivery → one review ask → incentive ৳50 off next order (disclosed on site — incentivized ≠ fake, hiding the incentive would be).

## Verification standard (all 3 required to publish)
1. Real order ID matches records. 2. Screenshot of the working product OR the chat thread. 3. Explicit consent message: "site-এ দেখাতে পারবেন" (kept on file).

## Publication format
First name + last initial, city optional, product, month. Full text unedited (typos kept — they read human because they are). NO star ratings until ≥25 published reviews (star schema stays banned by the auditor until then).

## Site integration (when ≥5 verified reviews exist)
Replace the third proof card on the homepage ("Reviews: earning them") with real entries + a "how we verify reviews" link — that page itself becomes a trust weapon nobody else has. Keep publishing the counts (1,600+ orders) alongside.

## Negative feedback rule
A complaint that we fixed within warranty MAY be published WITH the resolution ("seat died day 3 → replaced in 40 min") if the customer consents — fixed-problem stories convert better than praise. Never suppress; never argue publicly.

## Metrics (monthly, into the weekly review)
Review asks sent → responses → published · incentive redemptions · complaint-to-resolution median time (publish it once it's provably good).
